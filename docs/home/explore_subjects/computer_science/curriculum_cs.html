Studying Computer Science

Structure of studies

In computer science you are going to study in many different subjects. From more theoretical ones like theoretical computer science or linear algebra to more hands-on subjects like software internship. You have a wide variety of topics to choose from. The beginning of the studies is quite linear but in the later parts you can choose the subjects to your preference. You always have to get a certain number of credits but in the end itâ€™s up to you to create your custom study plan.

In the special study part in the last year of your studies you can choose your subjects from three different categories. The first one is more theoretical one called fundamentals of computer science. The second one is the hands-on software part called practical computer science. The last one is more hardware orienting and called practical technical computer science. You have to choose at least two subjects from each category but everyone has a lot of subjects and topics to choose form. You should be able to find a way you like.
