About Cyber Security

Cybersecurity refers to the practice of protecting internet-connected systems, including hardware, software and data, from attack, damage or unauthorized access. This includes protecting against a wide range of threats, such as hacking, malware, phishing, and even physical damage or theft. Cybersecurity is a critical concern for individuals, businesses, and governments, as the increasing reliance on technology has made it easier for cybercriminals to access sensitive information and disrupt operations. To combat these threats, organizations may use a variety of tools and strategies, including encryption, firewalls, intrusion detection and prevention systems, and employee training programs.
